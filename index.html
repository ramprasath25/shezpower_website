Who we are?

Shezpower is an exclusive business-networking platform for women entrepreneurs. This is the first online site in India where the products or services owned and operated by women entrepreneurs can be listed. Anyone can buy from our site, but sellers are women only! All you women entrepreneurs out there, why wait? Come and list your products and services with us! You’ll see how our site and programs can increase your ideas, opportunities and sales! Incase you’re still forming or creating your product and/or service ideas, we offer various training and skills development activities to help you achieve your goals. Our team of experts is ready to guide you in a range of ways.

Our mission

To support the entire business life-cycle for women entrepreneurs.

Our vision

To reach out to creative women all over India, and to offer them what they need to achieve their dreams as entrepreneurs.
